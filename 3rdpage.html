Diet 1 (weight gain)

Breakfast:

  • 2 whole grain toast slices with almond butter or peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of fortified soy milk or almond milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

  • Greek yogurt with honey and mixed berries Handful of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews)

    Lunch:

  • Veggie wrap with hummus, avocado, shredded carrots, spinach, and cucumber in a whole grain tortilla
  • Side of quinoa salad with mixed vegetables (bell peppers, tomatoes, corn, peas)
  • Apple slices with a tablespoon of almond butter for dipping

    Afternoon Snack:

  • Whole grain crackers with cheese slices or hummus Trail mix with dried fruits and nuts

    Dinner:

  • Baked sweet potato topped with black beans, diced tomatoes, avocado, and a sprinkle of cheese
  • Steamed broccoli with a drizzle of olive oil and lemon juice
  • Whole grain roll or a slice of whole grain bread

    Evening Snack:

  • Smoothie made with banana, berries, spinach, almond milk, and a scoop of protein powder or Greek yogurt

    Before Bed Snack (optional):

  • A small bowl of whole grain cereal with fortified soy milk or almond milk

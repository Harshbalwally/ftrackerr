Diet 2 (weight gain)

Breakfast:

  • Creamy oatmeal cooked with almond milk, topped with sliced bananas, chopped almonds, and a drizzle of honey
  • Scrambled eggs with diced bell peppers and spinach
  • 1 glass of fortified orange juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

  • Greek yogurt parfait layered with granola and mixed berries
  • Handful of trail mix (walnuts, dried cranberries, and pumpkin seeds)

    Lunch:

  • Veggie burger made with a black bean patty, whole grain bun, lettuce, tomato, avocado, and a side of sweet potato fries
  • Mixed green salad with cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and balsamic vinaigrette
  • 1 cup of fortified almond milk

    Afternoon Snack:

  • Hummus with whole grain pita bread or carrot sticks
  • 1 small apple with almond butter

    Dinner:

  • Stir-fried tofu and vegetable noodles with broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas, seasoned with a teriyaki sauce
  • Quinoa and chickpea salad with diced cucumbers, red onion, and lemon-tahini dressing
  • 1 serving of whole grain garlic bread
  • 1 cup of fortified soy milk

    Evening Snack:

  • Smoothie made with fortified almond milk, mixed berries, spinach, and a scoop of plant-based protein powder

    Before Bed:

  • A small bowl of cottage cheese with chopped nuts and a drizzle of honey

Diet 4 (weight gain)

Breakfast:

  • Overnight oats made with rolled oats, almond milk, chia seeds, sliced bananas, and a drizzle of maple syrup
  • Whole grain toast with avocado spread and sliced tomatoes
  • 1 glass of fortified orange juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

  • Greek yogurt with honey and mixed berries
  • Handful of mixed nuts (almonds, cashews, and walnuts)

    Lunch:

  • Black bean and sweet potato burrito bowl with brown rice, roasted corn, diced tomatoes, avocado slices, and salsa
  • Mixed green salad with chickpeas, cucumbers, and balsamic vinaigrette
  • 1 slice of whole grain bread
  • 1 cup of fortified almond milk

    Afternoon Snack:

  • Hummus with whole grain crackers or carrot sticks
  • 1 small apple

    Dinner:

  • Chickpea and vegetable coconut curry served with quinoa
  • Roasted cauliflower and broccoli
  • Mixed bean salad with bell peppers, onions, and lemon-tahini dressing
  • 1 serving of whole grain dinner roll
  • 1 cup of fortified soy milk

    Evening Snack:

  • Smoothie made with fortified almond milk, mixed berries, spinach, and a scoop of plant-based protein powder

    Before Bed:

  • A small bowl of Greek yogurt with chopped almonds and a drizzle of honey
